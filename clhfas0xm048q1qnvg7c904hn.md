I just saw [Andrés Arias](https://hashnode.com/@dresnite) post about how he has accepted the "[12 Startups in 12 Months](https://dresnite.com/im-launching-12-startups-in-12-months-in-2023)" challenge [that originated way back in 2014](https://levels.io/12-startups-12-months/).
